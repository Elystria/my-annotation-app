INSTRUCTIONS

Please outline the object in the image.
To do so, select the outline tool and click on the image where you want to start outlining. 
Click again when you're done.
If you are not satisfied with the outline, click on the trash and start again.

Click here to see a video demonstration.

HOW TO MAKE A GOOD OUTLINE

 1. The whole object has to be inside the outline. 
 2. The outline must follow roughly the shape of the image
 3. If there are more than one objects, outline only one.
